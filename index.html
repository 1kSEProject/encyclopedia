The Solar Era is marked by an asteroid impact so powerful it stopped the Earth's core. Forced to flee a thinning atmosphere humanity spread out amongst the planets. Over centuries our species evolved. Strange, new empires rose. A fragile peace has existed for a thousand years, but now old rivalries, new species, and the truth behind a mysterious black growth on the Sun threaten to unravel alliances and plunge the System into war. HISTORIES

Not much is not known about Earth before the Solar Era, but it is thought to have existed in a state of perpetual war. Evidence of this is twofold. First, countless references in ancient texts to massive arsenals of nuclear weapons held among nations. Second, a startling lack of ships capable of interplanetary travel. Modern historians believe terrestrial warfare and a ground-based economy are to blame for Humanity's tragic preparation and near extinction from the Impact.

Spectrum of Magics and Machines

Many different types of heroes and villains inhabit the worlds of 1kSE: amphibious knights, gravity mages, rogue surgeons, hacker trolls, dream weavers, and more. A person's skill and combat prowess is defined by the machine or magic they wield. The Earth was destroyed, and with it the secrets of technology. While some practices were remembered there still exists a schism of knowledge and faith. For every sword there is a staff, for every gun an incantation. This is the spectrum of magics and machines.
