INTRODUCTION

In order to understand the complex shifting of alliance and war that plague modern society we must first understand our history in the solar system at large. Much is not known about Earth before the Solar Era, but it is thought to have existed in a state of perpetual war. Evidence of this is twofold. First, the many references in ancient texts to the mass proliferation of nuclear weapons among nations. Second, a startling lack of interest in space travel and the solar system at large. Modern historians believe terrestrial warfare and a ground-based economy are to blame for Humanity’s tragic preparation and near extinction from The Impact.

While most of Earth’s history was lost to the void, recent expeditions have recovered records dating back to 7kTE. We owe an eternal debt in gratitude to the countless Warrior-Scholars who sacrificed their lives in the persuit of this knowledge; with it we now have a clearer picture of what Humanity was like during its Golden Age (as well as the events that led to it’s fall).
