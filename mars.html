MARS

OVERVIEW

Mars was the first extra-terrestrial planet inhabited by Man. The exact date the first colony appeared is unknown, but it was well before the process of terraforming worlds was adopted. As a result technologies similar to submarines and space ships were used to sustain life. Despite this, Mars thrived and grew into a massive city that spanned the entire globe. After the Impact Mars saw an unprecidented influx of refugees. Disease, famine, and crime were rampant. This drove many humans to abandon their bodies to be uploaded to the Internet (also known as the WWW). This created a cultural schism between digitized humans known as Electrists and the city-dwellers known as Outsiders which persists today.

PEOPLES

CIVILIZATIONS

WWW

Worlds Wide Web

The WWW is the main hub for offworlders and consists of many different worlds called "Servers" each with their own laws and physics. Depending on the Server's law, users can level up and gain items or abilities to assist with movement and combat. Between servers is the "Ether" where users are able to modify their code in real time and create their own servers.

IRL

The Sprawl

The Outside has a few safe areas for tourists to visit mars, but the vast majority of the planet is unregulated. The Syndicates rule the gritty alleys and run down buildings, while vigilante hackers keep them in check. In some of the more abandoned areas the air is toxic and so Oxygen is more precious than gold.

GOVERNMENT

SWARM

Mars as a society makes decisions by direct democracy. Votes are counted instantly by polling all users of the WWW. The speed at which this vote takes place allows Mars to analyse a situation, make decisions, and even hold conversation in real-time. The leader’s voice is a surreal amalgamation of all Electrists. They are known as a single entity, Swarm.
